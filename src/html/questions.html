Where can blockchain technology be used?

There can be a lot of applications in real life. In any service where they want to check something, most often you give unnecessary information about yourself.

Private payments

Unlike traditional payment structures that are visible to many parties, ZK-protected blockchain technology can validate transactions without the need to verify the underlying transaction data.

KYC

You can check your details without revealing anything extra.

Private social networks

You can build a social network on top of Aleo and make messages between users private.

Digital passport

If someone needs to check your age he can do this without finding out anything extra. (For example at the checkout in a store you will be asked for a document proving your age).

Driving license

If you are stopped by a traffic police inspector to check your license.

Digital Property

Can be purchased without disclosure.

AML

Anti Money Laundering is a system of measures against money laundering. You can verify the purity of receipt of your funds without disclosing any other data.
